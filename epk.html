Biography

Xac Anon was a lo-fi emo rapper from Australia whose music blended raw emotion with haunting production. His body of work — including *Purgatory*, *Second Chance*, and *Darkness and Light* — carries the echo of pain, growth, and transcendence. His legacy continues to inspire a generation of artists navigating their own purgatories.
