Web3 Digital Record Label.

We operate the full machinery of a major label: contracts, venues, media production, and marketing, reimagined for a decentralized era.

Business Architecture

Managing the critical path: high-level contracts, virtual venue management, 4K media production, and strategic sponsorships that bridge digital and physical markets.

The Passthrough Protocol

To attract and retain elite talent, BLK enforces 100% financial passthrough. Artists keep every cent of their Spotify, Apple Music, and physical merch revenue. We facilitate the growth, they keep the value.

Licensing & Likeness

Creating additional revenue streams through the licensing of artist avatars and music within the metaverse, vs. the "sink or swim" model of traditional labels that drain artist masters.

Sovereign Independence

We provide the marketing and production assistance that usually requires a major label, but leave the artist with the skills and ownership to remain truly independent.

Our in-house production collective manages the full creative pipeline, from composition to professional-grade vocal mixing and video production.

BLK leverages an expansive media network to drive ingress. We produce cinematic trailers and music videos promoted to our 1.44M Subscribers, ensuring project visibility long before users enter the Creator Cities.

This model facilitates high-performance development cycles and architectural world-building without interfering with the 100% Pass-Through distribution model.

The 70/30 Model

The 30% operational allotment is utilized exclusively for technical overhead: immersive hosting, world-building, and code maintenance.

Crucially, all external royalties and streaming revenue remain completely untouched by the protocol.

Vanilla.

BLK operates exclusively on the standard game client. By removing the requirement for external mods, we ensure 100% accessibility for the entire global user base.

Proprietary Asset Rendering

We render high-fidelity assets including custom branded apparel, vehicles, and companions directly within the vanilla environment via server-side injection.

Transportation Infrastructure

Native implementation of functional supercars, helicopters, and planes. Each asset provides high-utility movement within the Creator Cities.

Earth-Scale Mapping

The "Earth Render" project maps physical geography 1:1 into the grid, allowing artists to own and develop sovereign territories based on real-world locations.

Gameplay Customization

Minigames and gameplay loops are engineered to reflect the brand lore of specific projects, turning static environments into interactive gaming verticals.

EULA Compliance & Risk Mitigation

BLK operates under strict adherence to Microsoft/Mojang EULA and Commercial Usage Guidelines. All financial transactions occur on a proprietary off-game layer. No core gameplay features are locked behind paywalls, ensuring long-term platform stability.
