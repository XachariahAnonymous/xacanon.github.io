01 // Engineering

The Vanilla Protocol.

BLK operates exclusively on the standard game client. By removing the requirement for external mods or custom launchers, we ensure 100% accessibility for the global user base.

Proprietary Asset Rendering

We have engineered a method to render high-fidelity assets (including custom branded apparel, vehicles, and biological companions) directly within the vanilla environment. These are proprietary creations unique to the BLK platform.

Transportation Infrastructure

Native implementation of functional supercars, helicopters, and planes. Each asset is tailored to the project aesthetic and provides high-utility movement within Creator Cities.

Earth-Scale Mapping

The "Earth Render" project bridges physical geography with digital ownership. Large-scale sections of the physical world are mapped 1:1 into the grid, allowing artists to own and develop sovereign territories based on real-world locations.

Gameplay Customization

Bespoke minigames and gameplay loops are engineered to reflect the brand lore of specific projects, turning static environments into interactive gaming verticals.
