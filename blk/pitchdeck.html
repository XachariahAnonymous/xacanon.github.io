01 // Engineering

Vanilla.

BLK operates exclusively on the standard game client. By removing the requirement for external mods or custom launchers, we ensure 100% accessibility for the entire global user base.

Proprietary Asset Rendering

We have engineered a method to render high-fidelity assets including custom branded apparel, vehicles, and companions directly within the vanilla environment.

Transportation Infrastructure

Native implementation of functional supercars, helicopters, and planes. Each asset is tailored to the project aesthetic and provides high-utility movement within Creator Cities.

Earth-Scale Mapping

The "Earth Render" project bridges physical geography with digital ownership. Large-scale sections of the physical world are mapped 1:1 into the grid, allowing artists to own and develop sovereign territories based on real-world locations.

Gameplay Customization

Minigames and gameplay loops are engineered to reflect the brand lore of specific projects, turning static environments into interactive gaming verticals.

02 // Audio & Distribution

Sonic Engineering.

Our in-house production collective manages the full creative pipeline, from internal composition to professional-grade vocal mixing and video production.

100% Pass-Through Distribution

Unlike older systems, BLK operates on a Pass-Through Model. This means any value an artist creates outside of our direct platform—whether from services like Spotify and Apple Music, or from selling merchandise goes directly to them. BLK is not involved; we neither touch, tax, nor delay these external earnings, preserving the artist's full financial control..

BLK leverages an expansive media network to drive ingress. We produce cinematic trailers and music videos promoted to our 1.44M Subscribers, ensuring project visibility long before users enter the Creator Cities.

The 70/30 Model

The 30% operational allotment is utilized exclusively to facilitate the rigorous technical demands of the BLK environment. This includes localized management of immersive hosting, architectural world-building, and high-performance development cycles.

Crucially, this framework does not interfere with the BLK 100% Pass-Through distribution model; all external royalties and streaming revenue remain completely untouched by the protocol.
