THE FINE PRINT

  • You bring the cartridge. This site does not host, link to, or distribute ROM files of any kind.
  • It stays with you. Every ROM you drop in is stored only in your browser's local IndexedDB. There is no upload, no server, no database on our end.
  • Only dump what you own. Only use ROMs and BIOS files you've personally backed up from cartridges, discs, or consoles you legally own, where your local law permits it.
  • Emulation is legal. The emulator itself (EmulatorJS, open source) just runs game code, the same principle as any other software interpreter.
  • Clear it whenever. Use "Remove" on any cartridge or BIOS, or clear your browser's site data, to erase everything instantly.
