Project: Blue Book

SOP-99-ALPHA | OPERATIVE CONDUCT PROTOCOLS

Section I: Neutral Frequency
01. Political Neutrality

This frequency is reserved for the pursuit of the unknown. Discussions regarding real-world politics, candidates, or partisan movements are strictly prohibited to ensure the focus remains on the mission.

02. Inter-Agency Conduct

All operatives are expected to maintain professional decorum. We are a collective of truth-seekers; communication must remain constructive, respectful, and inclusive at all times.

03. Signal Clarity

Keep the comms clear for mission-critical data. Avoid spam, excessive capitalization, or the promotion of external servers and unrelated frequencies.

04. The Hecklefish Principle

Sarcasm and wit are inherent to our culture, but they must never compromise the safety or well-being of a fellow operative. If a joke becomes a breach of respect, it is no longer permitted.

Section II: Site Integrity
05. Site Authorization

Do not tamper with, enter, or modify a Research Site (base) that is not your own without explicit authorization from the lead operative of that site.

06. Structural Stability

To maintain server equilibrium, do not construct "Lag Machines" or unoptimized redstone clocks that could destabilize the simulation for other operatives.

Section III: Tech Protocols
07. Standard Issue Only

Operatives must use approved clients. The use of unauthorized augmentations—such as X-ray, flight-hacks, or automated combat tools—is a direct breach of the project's mechanical integrity.

08. Anomaly Reporting

If you discover a "glitch in the matrix" (duplication or economy exploit), you are required to report it to a Lead Researcher immediately rather than utilizing it for personal gain.

Section IV: Security (OPSEC)
09. Identity Protection

Never share real-world coordinates or personal data of yourself or other operatives on public frequencies. Protect the identity of the Division.

10. Account Integrity

You are the sole custodian of your access credentials. All actions taken under your operative ID are your sole responsibility.
