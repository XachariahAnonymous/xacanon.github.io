Server Protocol

FILE ID: WF-MC-001

01. Code of Conduct

Be safe, be kind, and show respect to all investigators. Toxic behavior is a simulation anomaly that will be purged.

02. Unfair Advantage

The use of third-party modifications, hacks, or exploits to gain an advantage is strictly prohibited. We are here to study the simulation, not break it.

03. Intel Integrity

Do not impersonate staff or spread misinformation regarding server events. Keep the archive clean and accurate.

Violations of protocol will result in immediate termination of access.
